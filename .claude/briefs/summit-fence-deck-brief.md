## Design Decisions
- **Hero concept:** curtain-split-reveal — Full-dark viewport with vertical split down centre, two "curtain" panels pulled apart revealing business name in the gap. Spotlight gradient from above illuminates the text. Tagline in small caps with wide letter-spacing below. Deep crimson background, slightly darker at edges.
- **Signature moves (3):**
  1. **spotlight-sections** — Sections with a radial gradient highlight illuminating key content from above, like a stage spotlight, with surrounding area slightly darker. Used on: signature-project section, materials/quality section.
  2. **iron-stamp-badge** — Small stamped verification badges on testimonial cards and service cards. Inline flex with accent border, check icon, "Verified" or "5-Year Warranty" text. Used on: testimonials, services.
  3. **jagged-wave-divider** — Dramatic SVG section dividers with jagged/wave curves between sections. Varied shapes per divider instance. Used on: between all major sections.
- **Section order:** Premium Showcase
  1. hero
  2. signature-project (featured backyard transformation)
  3. services (5 core services)
  4. process (4-step: consult → design → build → enjoy)
  5. materials (cedar, composite, aluminum quality showcase)
  6. testimonials
  7. cta
- **Animation patterns:**
  - Hero: clip-reveal + parallax (background)
  - Signature-project: blur-sharpen (premium reveal)
  - Services: slide-left/slide-right alternating
  - Process: fade-up + cascade (sequential steps)
  - Materials: scale-up + fan (grow to emphasize quality)
  - Testimonials: rotate-in + wave (playful cards)
  - CTA: none (intentional stillness)
- **Inner page layouts:**
  - Services: C (Sidebar Navigation — premium feel, lots of detail per service)
  - About: C (Split Hero + Values Grid — owner story + company values)
  - Contact: A (Form + Sidebar — form left, contact info + map right)
